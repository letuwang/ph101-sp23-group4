Our Mission!

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Our mission is to make a positive impact on the environment by reducing paper waste in the Berkeley area. Our goal is to promote sustainable practices and provide solutions that are accessible to everyone in the community.

One of our primary focuses is on establishing cardboard box reusing centers in key areas, such as the MLK's Amazon Locker Room area and residential housing's mailing rooms, to make it more convenient for UC Berkeley students, faculty, and staff to recycle and reuse cardboard boxes. We believe that by incentivizing the return and recycling of packaging materials, we can encourage more people to participate in recycling and reusing efforts.

We recognize that overconsumption of paper is one of the major drivers of climate change, resulting in greenhouse gas emissions and deforestation. Therefore, we aim to work with UC Berkeley, a campus with a population of over 40,000 students, faculty, and staff, to reduce paper waste. By focusing on UC Berkeley, we can make a significant impact on the environment and set an example for other institutions to follow.

In addition, the city of Berkeley itself is a relevant geographic area to focus on. We plan to work with the city to establish more sustainable practices and encourage residents to recycle and reuse paper products.

Together, we can make a difference by reducing paper waste and promoting sustainable practices in the Berkeley community.
