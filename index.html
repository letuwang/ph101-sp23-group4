RE-USE CARDBOARD BOXES!

Some Stats!

  • Approximately 68% of all paper consumed in the United States in 2021 is used for packaging and cardboard purposes.
  • The United States alone produces 51 million metric ton of wrapping and packaging paper annually, which is equivalent to approximately 184,000,000 cardboard boxes.
  • Another 3.3 million metric tons of packaging paper and paperboard were imported into the United States.

Paper is not the solution

The impact of paper waste on climate change is significant due to several reasons. Firstly, paper waste constitutes a staggering 26% of the total waste present in landfills, which in turn contributes to air pollution. Additionally, the primary production of paper involves cutting down trees, leading to deforestation, which has adverse effects on the environment and contributes to global warming. Therefore, reducing paper waste is crucial in mitigating the negative effects of climate change. Learn more.

Most of consumed paper is packaging related

Cardboard and packing material make up around half of all paper consumed in the US.
